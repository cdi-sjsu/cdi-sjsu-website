TinyTapeout Resource Doc

The big public spreadsheet behind this curated list, with many more links for deeper exploration.

ASIC pathway

TinyTapeout

Open silicon submissions, docs, specs, workshops, and a practical route to physical ASIC chips.

logic sim

Wokwi TinyTapeout Intro

Browser-based digital logic design and simulation for a first TinyTapeout-style circuit.

silicon layout

SiliWiz

Draw doped silicon, polysilicon, and metal layers in-browser to see how transistors behave.

Verilog practice

HDLBits

Short HDL exercises for combinational logic, sequential logic, state machines, and modules.

digital logic

NandGame

Interactive logic design practice that starts from NAND gates and builds toward a small computer.

browser EDA

Makerchip

Browser-based HDL tools for writing, simulating, and sharing digital design experiments.

simulation

EDA Playground

Online Verilog/SystemVerilog/VHDL editor and simulator, useful for quickly sharing testbenches.

testbenches

cocotb

Python-based verification for HDL designs, especially useful before a TinyTapeout submission.

OpenLane 2

Open-source flow documentation for turning RTL into a placed and routed chip layout.

synthesis

Yosys

Open-source Verilog synthesis for understanding what RTL becomes before layout.

GTKWave

Waveform viewer for inspecting simulations and debugging signal behavior over time.

Schematic-based logic design tool with examples, test cases, K-maps, and simulation features.

8bitworkshop

Browser templates and examples for experimenting with Verilog projects.

FPGA4Fun

Practical Verilog examples for display, game, and hardware-oriented FPGA projects.
